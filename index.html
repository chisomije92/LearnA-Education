Graphic Design

The course covers various aspects of graphic design, including branding, advertising, digital media, and print materials. Additionally, students explore design trends, historical influences, and industry best practices.

  • Teaches fundamentals of graphic design
  • Covers typography and layout principles
  • Explores Adobe Creative Suite tools

Psychology

The course covers various areas such as cognitive, behavioral, social, and developmental psychology. Students learn about the basic principles of human behavior, including perception, memory, learning, motivation, and emotion.

  • Teaches basics of psychological theories
  • Covers cognitive and behavioral psychology

Photography

The photography course covers fundamental principles and techniques of photography, including camera operation, composition, lighting, and post-processing. Learners explore various genres such as portrait, landscape, and street photography, gaining practical skills through hands-on exercises and projects.

  • Teaches all about camera settings such as aperture, shutter speed, and ISO
  • Teaches the rule of thirds and other compositional guidelines
  • Exploring different photography styles and genres (portrait, landscape, street, wildlife, etc.)
